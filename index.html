Quantifying Patterns: Missed Trash and Recycling Collection

In this study, I conducted an in-depth analysis of the trash collection points that were missed in the 8 wards of Washington D.C. This analysis covered a period of three months and utilized spatial analysis techniques. The findings of the study pinpointed the specific wards that required immediate attention due to missed trash collection, as well as the spatial relationships and patterns within the area.

Tools Used: ArcGIS Online, ArcGIS StoryMaps

About Me

As a passionate Geospatial Analyst, I excel in transforming spatial data into actionable insights. My expertise lies in leveraging tools like ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, ArcGIS StoryMaps and GEODA to analyze and visualize geographic data. I thrive on solving intricate spatial problems and have a proven track record of delivering high-impact solutions. My best qualities are my analytical mindset, attention to detail, and a relentless drive for innovation in every project I undertake.
